Summer Paradise

Why spend a fortune in going to Hawaii during the summer, when you can experience summer in your own backyard. the Summer Paradise is the worlds biggest Indoor beach, Slide in one. Are you a parent with a dire need of a vacation or some time in the sun and want a break from the baby sitting? With a slide of a height of a 100m, you need not worry about your kids running about the park as they would be too busy playing around in the slides, or in the beach where they can replicate whatever that they would do on a normal beach day.
